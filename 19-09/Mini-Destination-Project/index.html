Quick Travel Tips

  • Keep cash handy, as rural areas may not accept cards/UPI.
  • Carry a reusable water bottle to avoid dehydration.
  • Respect dress codes while visiting temples and mosques.
  • Try local street food from pani puri to dosa.
  • Always bargain politely in local markets.
